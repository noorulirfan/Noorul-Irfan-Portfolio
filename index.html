About Me

I initially wondered how I would fit into the world of education as a teacher, but once I stepped into this wonderful world, I realized that teaching is the greatest profession in the world. This was my precious teaching practice. After completing some observations, I changed my perspective and recognized my teaching skills. I was able to learn many things that will be helpful to me when I become a certified teacher one day.

Although I could face many possible challenges in the classroom, I have many of the strengths it takes to be a good teacher. There were many times during this field training that gave me a broad idea of how the ordinary life of a teacher would go. Similarly, I got a profound sense of how the classroom environment is for most instructors. In general, I believe this was a pleasant experience for me, and I learned a lot in such a brief period.

I have been working as a web developer for quite some time now. I believe I am a very ambitious person who loves to work on making the website work well. Working on the web is my passion as I love to work on exciting projects. This is the field I get to express my creativity.
